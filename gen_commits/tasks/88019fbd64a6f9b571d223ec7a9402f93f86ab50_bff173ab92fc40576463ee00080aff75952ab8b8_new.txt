refactor(themes): Standardize light background color

Removed custom `grey_50`, `grey_statusbar`, and `drawer_background_light` color resources.
Replaced references to `grey_50` with `android.R.color.white` in `ThemeCache` and `WidgetTheme`.
Introduced `md_background_light` to consolidate light theme background references in `colors.xml`.
Updated `window_background`, `content_background`, `dialog_background`, and `drawer_color` to use `md_background_light`.
This simplifies the color palette and promotes consistency for light theme elements.
