feat: Support multiline subtask titles

Enable multiline text input for new subtasks and display for existing ones
in the subtask editor.

The layout of both `NewSubtaskRow` and `ExistingSubtaskRow` has been
adjusted to align elements (checkbox, text field) to the top and ensure
proper padding when subtask titles span multiple lines. The `TextField`
for new subtasks now uses `singleLine = false` and `maxLines = Int.MAX_VALUE`.
