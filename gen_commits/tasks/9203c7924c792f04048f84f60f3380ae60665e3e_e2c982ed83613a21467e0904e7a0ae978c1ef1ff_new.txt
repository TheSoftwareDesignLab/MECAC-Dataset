feat(nb): Improve task terminology to 'gjøremål' and 'gjøremålssteg'

The Norwegian translation previously used "oppgaver" for tasks and "underoppgaver" for subtasks. This commit updates the terminology for better clarity and consistency:
- "gjøremål" for "tasks" (e.g., "Importer gjøremål", "Slett dette gjøremålet").
- "gjøremålssteg" for "subtasks" (e.g., "Legg til gjøremålssteg", "Utvid gjøremålssteg").

Also includes minor wording adjustments for better flow, such as replacing "påminner deg om" with "minner deg om" and "tømme" with "fjerne" in certain contexts.
