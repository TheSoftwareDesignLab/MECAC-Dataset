feat(login): Track signup cancellation, email, Google, and TOS taps

Add new analytics tracking methods to the LoginAnalyticsListener interface.
These methods capture user interactions during the signup process:
- trackSignupCanceled(): Tracks when a user cancels the signup process.
- trackSignupEmailButtonTapped(): Tracks taps on the email signup button.
- trackSignupGoogleButtonTapped(): Tracks taps on the Google signup button.
- trackSignupTermsOfServiceTapped(): Tracks taps on the Terms of Service link.
