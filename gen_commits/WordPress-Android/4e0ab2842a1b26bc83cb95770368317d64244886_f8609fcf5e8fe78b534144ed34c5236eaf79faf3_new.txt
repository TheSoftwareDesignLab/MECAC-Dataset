refactor(aztec): Adapt to Aztec 1.0-beta.2 image API

Upgrade Aztec library to v1.0-beta.2 and adapt the editor to its updated image-specific APIs.
This involves renaming `OnMediaTappedListener` to `OnImageTappedListener`, `setOnMediaTappedListener` to `setOnImageTappedListener`, `insertMedia` to `insertImage`, and `mediaTapped` to `onImageTapped`.
The changes refine the distinction between generic media and images within the Aztec editor.
