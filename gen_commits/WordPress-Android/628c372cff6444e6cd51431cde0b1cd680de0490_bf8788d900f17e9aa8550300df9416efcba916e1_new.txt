refactor(fastlane): Streamline metadata downloads and en-US source handling

Consolidates the separate `download_wordpress_metadata_strings` and
`download_jetpack_metadata_strings` lanes into a single, more generic
`download_metadata_strings` lane.

This change also simplifies the handling of `en-US` metadata by:
* Removing the unused `en-gb` locale from `WP_RELEASE_NOTES_LOCALES`.
* Directly copying source metadata files into the `en-US` directory,
  replacing the previous, app-specific fallback mechanisms.

Introduces `display_name` in `APP_SPECIFIC_VALUES` for improved logging
and commit messages.
