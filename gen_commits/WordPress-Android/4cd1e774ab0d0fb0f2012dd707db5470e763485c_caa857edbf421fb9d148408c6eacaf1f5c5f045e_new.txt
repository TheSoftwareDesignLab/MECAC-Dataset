refactor: Remove Quick Actions Card focus points

This commit removes the quick start focus points feature from the My Site Quick Actions Card. The change includes:
*   Deletion of `showStatsFocusPoint` and `showPagesFocusPoint` from data models.
*   Removal of `QuickStartFocusPoint` UI elements from `quick_actions_card.xml`.
*   Cleanup of related parameters, logic, and unit tests across ViewModel, builders, and views.
