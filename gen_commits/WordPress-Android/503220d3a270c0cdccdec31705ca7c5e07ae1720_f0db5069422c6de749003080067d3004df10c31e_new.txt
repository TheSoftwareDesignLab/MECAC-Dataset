style: Remove divider from stats widget list items

The visual divider separating the stat period and value has been removed from all stats widget list items (Today, All-Time, Views).

This involved:
- Deleting the `divider` TextView from `stats_views_widget_item_dark.xml` and `stats_views_widget_item_light.xml`.
- Removing all related visibility logic in `AllTimeWidgetListProvider`, `TodayWidgetListProvider`, and `ViewsWidgetListProvider`.
- Removing the `showDivider` property from `ViewsWidgetListViewModel.UiModel`.
- Adjusting layout attributes (`layout_width`, `layout_weight`) for other elements in the widget item layouts.
- Removing `android:gravity` from the `StatsChange` style due to layout restructuring.
