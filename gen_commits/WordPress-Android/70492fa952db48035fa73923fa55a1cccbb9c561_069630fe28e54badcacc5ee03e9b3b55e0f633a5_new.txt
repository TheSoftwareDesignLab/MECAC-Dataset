feat: Add optional icon to Jetpack backup footnote item

The `jetpack_backup_restore_list_footnote_item.xml` layout is updated to support an optional icon, displayed alongside the footnote text.

- An `ImageView` is introduced, initially hidden, to render the icon.
- ConstraintLayout chains are utilized to align the icon and footnote text horizontally.
- The `Jetpack.Backup.ListItem.Footnote` style is adjusted, setting the text width to `0dp` for flexible sizing within the chain and adding `lineSpacingMultiplier`.
- A new `Jetpack.Backup.ListItem.Footnote.Icon` style is created for icon styling.
- Item container padding is also standardized.
