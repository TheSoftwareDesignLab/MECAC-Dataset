feat(cakepay): Introduce Cake Pay for in-app card purchases

This release replaces the previous Ionia gift card integration with a new Cake Pay service.

Cake Pay offers functionality to purchase worldwide prepaid and gift cards directly within the app, featuring a redesigned user experience.

Key changes include:
- Complete removal of all Ionia-related API, service, data models, UI components, and routing.
- Integration of the new Cake Pay API, service layer, data models, and MobX view models.
- Addition of dedicated UI screens for Cake Pay account management, card browsing, and purchasing.
- New secrets added to CI for Cake Pay API keys and authorization tokens.
- Localization updates for new features and improved descriptions.
