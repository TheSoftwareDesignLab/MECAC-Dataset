feat: Add single tap map object identification

Implements onSingleTapUp gesture handling to identify map symbols
at the tapped location. This enables retrieving detailed information
about markers, amenities, and map objects displayed on the map.

- Retrieves MapSymbolInformationList for the touch area.
- Dynamically casts symbols to IBillboardMapSymbol.
- Extracts LatLon coordinates for the identified symbol.
- Identifies and logs MapMarker (including context pin), Amenity,
  MapObject, and ObfMapObject details for debugging purposes.
