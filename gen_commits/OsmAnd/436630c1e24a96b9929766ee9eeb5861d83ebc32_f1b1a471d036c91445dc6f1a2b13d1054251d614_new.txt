feat: Enhance MGRS coordinate display with custom formatting

Introduces new methods `toFlavoredString()` and `toFlavoredString(int accuracy)` in MGRSPoint.
These methods format MGRS coordinates with spaces for readability and allow specifying the desired precision, handling grid square shifts on rounding.
All MGRS displays in the application are updated to use `toFlavoredString(5)` for 1-meter resolution.
