feat: Introduce modular bottom sheet menu system

Refactors `MenuBottomSheetDialogFragment` to use a list of `BaseBottomSheetItem`
objects, allowing dynamic content generation. This creates a flexible and
extensible bottom sheet UI, simplifying dialog creation and customization.

- Added `bottom_sheet_menu_base.xml` as a generic base layout.
- `MenuBottomSheetDialogFragment` now dynamically inflates items from its `items` list.
- Introduced `BaseBottomSheetItem` and subclasses (`SimpleBottomSheetItem`,
  `BottomSheetItemWithDescription`, `DividerHalfBottomSheetItem`) to encapsulate
  item-specific layout and logic.
- Migrated `AddWaypointBottomSheetDialogFragment` to use the new modular system,
  deleting its dedicated layout `fragment_add_waypoint_bottom_sheet_dialog.xml`.
- Updated `bottom_sheet_item_title.xml` to ensure consistent min height.
