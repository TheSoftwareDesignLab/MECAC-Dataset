build: Migrate app name and edition to resValue

Replaces custom Gradle tasks and string replacement logic with `resValue` declarations within product flavors.

This simplifies the build process by:
- Removing `updateNoTranslate` and `cleanNoTranslate` tasks.
- Deleting the `replaceNoTranslate` function.
- Consolidating app name and edition definition via `resValue` in `build.gradle`.
- Cleaning up `no_translate.xml` and `.gitignore` entries.
- Removing an unused Facebook SDK ApplicationId from `AndroidManifest-nightlyFree.xml`.
