feat: Add themable background color to navigation arrow

Introduces a new theme attribute `nav_arrow_circle_color` to control the fill
color of the navigation arrow's background circle.

- Defined `nav_arrow_circle_color_light` and `nav_arrow_circle_color_dark` in colors.xml.
- Mapped these colors to the new attribute in osmand_light_style.xml and
  osmand_dark_style.xml respectively.
- Updated TurnDrawable to use this theme attribute for the fill paint
  of the turn outlay and to dynamically update the color when night mode changes.
- Ensures NextTurnBaseWidget updates TurnDrawable's night mode state.
- Minor refactoring in MapInfoLayer for consistent color retrieval.
