feat: Introduce custom ListPopupWindow for simple menus

Refactors simple popup menu displays by introducing a new utility
`UiUtilities.createListPopupWindow`. This replaces `androidx.appcompat.widget.PopupMenu`
with a custom `ListPopupWindow` implementation using `SimplePopUpMenuItemAdapter`
and `popup_menu_item.xml`. This provides more control over menu item
layout and behavior.

Initially applied to chart axis selection in `TrackDetailsMenu` for
both Y and X axes.
