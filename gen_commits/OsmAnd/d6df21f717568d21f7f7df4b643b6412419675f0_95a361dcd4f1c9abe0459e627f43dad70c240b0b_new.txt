feat: Add timestamp and display date for download items

Introduce an abstract `getTimestamp(boolean remote)` method to `DownloadItem` to standardize date retrieval. `MultipleDownloadItem` now calculates its timestamp based on the latest child item. The `ItemViewHolder` now displays this date in the item description. The `UpdatesIndexFragment` is refactored to use `DownloadItem`s, enabling grouped items to be shown in the updates list with correct date information.
