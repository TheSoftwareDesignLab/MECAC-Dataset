refactor: Consolidate layout dimensions and enhance RTL support

Replaced numerous hardcoded dp values for layout margins and paddings with `@dimen` resources to ensure consistent spacing throughout the UI.
Implemented `android:layout_marginStart`, `android:layout_marginEnd`, `android:paddingStart`, `android:paddingEnd`, `android:drawableStart`, `android:drawableEnd`, and `android:gravity="start/end"` attributes to provide robust Right-to-Left (RTL) language support for all affected layouts.
