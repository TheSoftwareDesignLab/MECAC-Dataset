refactor: Improve RTL support by using start/end attributes

Migrates layout attributes and drawer logic to use 'start' instead of 'left'.
This includes:
- Updating `DrawerLayout` and its components (ListView, RelativeLayout) in `main.xml`.
- Changing `TextView` padding and drawable attributes in `simple_list_menu_item.xml`.
- Replacing `Gravity.LEFT` with `Gravity.START` in `MapActivity.java` for drawer operations.
This ensures proper display and behavior in Right-To-Left (RTL) locales.
