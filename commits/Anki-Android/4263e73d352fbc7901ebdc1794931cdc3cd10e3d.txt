Change night themes accent color

In order to pass accessibility checker when comparing text to background (contrast > 4.5)

theme_dark_accent and theme_black_accent attributes were removed in favor of accessing the color directly with `?attr/colorAccent`, which simplifies the code and make improve its fidelity with the themes

Dark theme: Material blue 400 (https://material.io/resources/color/#!/?view.left=0&view.right=0&primary.color=42A5F5)

Black theme: Material blue 500
