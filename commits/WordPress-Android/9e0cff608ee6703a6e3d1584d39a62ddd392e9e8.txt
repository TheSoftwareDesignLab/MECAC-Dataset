Wrap ConstraintLayout inside a NestedScrollView

ConstraintLayout as a direct child of the CoordinatorLayout doesn't seem to scroll properly according to the appbar scrolling view behavior. Nesting it within a NestedScrollView and applying the appbar scrolling view behavior to it seems to resolve the issue.
