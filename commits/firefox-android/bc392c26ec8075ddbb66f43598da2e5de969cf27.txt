So we're casting to `Collection` to filter but casting back to `List` to store

It looks like others have seen this problem: here's [an issue filed for
findbugs](https://youtrack.jetbrains.com/issue/KT-18311) and [the issue it was
duped to](https://youtrack.jetbrains.com/issue/KT-16446). Neither appears to be
verified by the core maintainers or prioritized (the original issue is filed in
Feb 2017).

I think the best course of action here is to suppress the error.
