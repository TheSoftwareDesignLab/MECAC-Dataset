Created a new, minimal gallery class specifically for the header:
WMFArticleHeaderGalleryViewController. It shares a parent class with the
original gallery: WMFPageCollectionViewController, which handles all
business logic for maintaining a "currentPage" property.

The article header gallery is embedded into the article VC's
tableheaderview. For MVP, the fullscreen gallery is presented modally
(as before) but we still hope to do a nicer transition in the near
future.

Also using a promise for the article request to allow presenting the
gallery while the mobileview data is still being loaded.

    sufficient)
